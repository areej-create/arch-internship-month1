Bean Connect — Coffee Ordering App

Dec 2025 Freelance Mobile

A full end-to-end mobile app UI/UX design for a premium coffee ordering platform — Bean Connect. The brief called for a warm, inviting aesthetic that made ordering coffee feel as good as drinking it. Designed entirely in Figma, with a complete design system and 10+ unique screens.

  • Designed end-to-end mobile UI including onboarding splash, authentication (login/signup), home feed, product catalog, item detail, cart, rewards system, and order tracking screens.
  • Created a consistent design system with a warm brown and cream color palette, custom typography pairing, and a full component library for scalable development handoff.
  • Crafted high-fidelity interactive prototypes in Figma simulating real app navigation and micro-interaction patterns for review and testing.
  • Optimized all user flows for conversion — from first launch to completed order — reducing friction at every key decision point.

NIWA — Color Analysis Platform

Dec 2025 Freelance Web

NIWA is a premium personal color analysis brand — "The Alchemy of You." The platform helps users discover their seasonal color palette (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter) to guide fashion, beauty, and personal styling. The brief required an editorial, high-end aesthetic with a warm and scientific feel.

  • Designed a full multi-section editorial landing page featuring a cinematic hero, seasonal color palette showcases, methodology explanation, palette card section, and community testimonials.
  • Built a complete Figma design system with reusable components including hero sections, palette cards, feature grids, testimonial carousels, and CTA banners.
  • Maintained a warm, cream and deep purple visual language aligned with NIWA's premium, science-meets-beauty brand positioning throughout all sections.
  • Structured content hierarchy and user flow to guide visitors naturally from awareness → education → conversion (palette discovery service).

Demoxis — Civic Issue Resolution

Feb 2026 Freelance Web

Demoxis is a civic technology platform connecting verified citizens with local councillors and MPs to resolve real-world issues — from submission through to visible resolution. The platform serves three distinct user types (Citizens, Councillors, MPs) and required a trusted, institutional aesthetic with clear information architecture.

  • Designed a full marketing website UI with structured sections including hero, problem statement, how it works (5-step flow), audience-specific feature breakdowns, projects map, and footer.
  • Created distinct visual flows for three user personas — Citizens, Councillors, and MPs — each with their own feature section, value proposition, and CTA button.
  • Applied a teal and white design system that communicates civic trust, government authority, and digital clarity — avoiding both corporate coldness and consumer playfulness.
  • Structured the information architecture to take a visitor from awareness to sign-up with zero ambiguity, using progressive disclosure across page sections.
